Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Nexis Infinity and our autonomous technology.

How does Offline AI work without internet? +
Nexis bundles highly quantized, optimized Small Language Models (SLMs) directly into the app structure. By utilizing your device's native CPU and NPU architecture via C++ bindings, it computes neural responses locally without transferring bytes over the network.
Who is the developer behind Nexis Infinity? +
Nexis Infinity was founded and developed entirely by Krishna Prajapati, a full-stack software engineer and AI researcher dedicated to building privacy-first autonomous consumer software.
Is my personal data and phone automation secure? +
Yes. We employ a zero-knowledge architecture. Local actions stay on device. When using OpenRouter online models, API transmissions are encrypted using TLS 1.3, and we never store your personal prompts or memory graphs on our servers.
How do I configure OpenRouter AI models? +
Simply navigate to the Settings tab within the Nexis app, select 'Cloud Providers', and paste your personal OpenRouter API key. You will instantly gain access to GPT-4o, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Gemini Pro, and Llama 3 models.
